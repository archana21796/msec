Why MSEC

We are autonomous Students can choose what & when they want to learn MSEC’s autonomous status has allowed us to create a Choice-based Credit System similar to the culture in top international universities. This system enables students to choose subjects that align with their passion and industry’s requirements, such as Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, Design Thinking and Prototyping, and much more. There are also special elective courses to transform Engineers into Managers, like Entrepreneurship Development, Financial statement analysis, Corporate Finance, Securities Market, Managerial Economics and Project Management. So finally, you have the freedom to select what you want to learn, and study at your own pace.

Placements

Best Engineering College with Placements At MSEC, placements are a priority. We take pride in maintaining over 90.58% placement rates, especially in core engineering sectors. Our dedicated placement cell works closely with leading recruiters like TCS, Infosys, Zoho, and core companies, ensuring that every eligible student is career-ready. Our central Chennai location helps students gain direct access to top corporate networks and internship opportunities.
