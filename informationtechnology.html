Information Technology Department

Department of Information Technology

Vision

Produce technically savvy students, encourage innovative skills and inculcate moral and professional values.

Mission

  • Enrich knowledge through stakeholder interaction.
  • Promote innovation in IT for research and development.
  • Mould students with ethical and moral values.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

  • Utilize science and IT in engineering applications.
  • Apply computing and telecom skills in enterprises.
  • Solve technical problems through logical thinking.
  • Develop systems with societal awareness.
  • Gain employment and solve real-world problems.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  • Develop optimal IT business solutions.
  • Provide socially responsible IT solutions.
  • Lead teams with ethics and management skills.

Program Outcomes (POs)

  • PO1: Apply engineering knowledge.
  • PO2: Analyze and solve complex problems.
  • PO3: Design solutions with societal concerns.
  • PO4: Conduct meaningful research.
  • PO5: Use modern tools responsibly.
  • PO6: Address societal and legal issues.
  • PO7: Emphasize environmental sustainability.
  • PO8: Uphold ethical standards.
  • PO9: Work effectively in teams.
  • PO10: Communicate proficiently.
  • PO11: Apply project and financial management.
  • PO12: Pursue lifelong learning.

2021 Regulation PEOs

  • Demonstrate competence and critical thinking.
  • Exhibit leadership and entrepreneurship skills.
  • Be responsible in social and environmental contexts.
  • Adapt ethically to complex challenges.
  • Embrace lifelong learning and innovation.

2021 Regulation PSOs

  • Proficient in programming and problem-solving.
  • Build automated business solutions with tech.
  • Engage in research with applied computing focus.
